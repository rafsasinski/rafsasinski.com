CASE STUDY

Spharce Logo

Personal Concept

Spharce is a personal logo concept created during my early exploration of vector graphics, symbolism, and brand identity.

Role
Logo Design
Project Type
Personal Project
Tools
Inkscape
Period
2024
Influences
Chakra, Travel, Planets

The Story Behind the Logo

Early discovery
I was amazed that vector graphics were not limited by resolution. When I discovered Inskape, it felt like a new creative world had opened up.
Concept & symbolism
The logo was inspired by the body’s seven main chakras and ideas of energy, balance, movement and duality.
Form & feeling
The circles feel like energy points, planets, or bubbles moving through an invisible universe. It was simple, but it had rhythm.
Name origin
The name Spharce was influenced by childhood trips to Austria with my father. Memories of Sparkasse and Spar somehow merged in my head, shaping the name.
Reflection
Looking back, Spharce represents curiosity, memory, travel, and the early creative play that made me fall in love with design tools.

  • Design Approach

    Minimal and geometric. Built from circles to express flow, connection and clarity without overcomplicating the mark.

  • Visual Language

    Circles are the core element. Their rhythm and scale create a feeling of movement, balance and quiet energy.

  • What It Means to Me

    It reminds me of where my creative journey started: breaking things, exploring tools, and learning by making.
